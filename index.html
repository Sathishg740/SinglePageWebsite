PUBG

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, better known as PUBG , is a multiplayer battle royale game in which players drop onto an island and fight to be the last one left standing.









There are currently three maps: Erangel, Sanhok, and Miramar. You can choose whether to play solo, duo, or with a squad of up to four players.
